# ADVANCED CHART CHECKLIST (2026)
# Expanded Buy Signal System
# Source: User's advanced chart reading checklist, December 2025

## ADVANCED BUY CHECKLIST
- □ Price above VWAP?
- □ Price above 9 EMA?
- □ 9 EMA above 21 EMA?
- □ Price above 50 & 200 SMA?
- □ RSI between 40-70?
- □ MACD blue above orange?
- □ Bollinger: Not already at upper band for too long?

### SCORING
- 6-7 CHECKS = STRONG BUY
- 4-5 CHECKS = DECENT, but wait for more confirmation
- 0-3 CHECKS = AVOID

## HOW TO USE
- Run this checklist before every trade
- Only act when most conditions are met
- Use as a supplement to the 3-line rule for higher conviction
