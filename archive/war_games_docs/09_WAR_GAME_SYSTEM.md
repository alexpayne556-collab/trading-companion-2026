# TRADING WAR GAME SYSTEM
## Pre-Trade Scenario Analysis - Run Every Outcome Before You Risk Money

---

## THE CONCEPT

Before every trade, you WAR GAME it. You ask:

**"What are ALL the possible outcomes, and what do I do in EACH scenario?"**

This eliminates emotion. When the scenario happens, you've already decided what to do.

---

## THE 6 SCENARIOS (Every Trade Has These)

```
SCENARIO 1: PERFECT TRADE
Price goes straight to target. Everything works.

SCENARIO 2: MESSY WIN  
Price chops around, tests your stop, then goes to target.

SCENARIO 3: PARTIAL WIN
Price goes halfway to target, then reverses. You get some profit.

SCENARIO 4: BREAKEVEN
Price goes up a bit, you move stop to breakeven, it comes back and stops you out.

SCENARIO 5: SMALL LOSS
Your stop gets hit. You lose your planned risk amount. Trade didn't work.

SCENARIO 6: GAP DOWN / BLACK SWAN
Something unexpected happens. Price gaps through your stop. Bigger loss than planned.
```

**You must have a plan for ALL SIX before you enter.**

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    SCENARIO PLANNING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SCENARIO 1: PERFECT TRADE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What happens: Price goes directly to Target 2
My action: Sell 50% at Target 1, 50% at Target 2
Result: Full profit captured
Probability: ~20%

SCENARIO 2: MESSY WIN
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What happens: Price dips near stop, scares me, then rallies to target
My action: Trust my stop, don't panic sell, let it work
Result: Full profit but with stress
Probability: ~15%
WARNING: Do NOT move stop further away when this happens

SCENARIO 3: PARTIAL WIN
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What happens: Price goes to Target 1, fails to reach Target 2, reverses
My action: Sell 50% at Target 1, stop remaining at breakeven
Result: Half profit captured, half at breakeven = still a win
Probability: ~20%

SCENARIO 4: BREAKEVEN
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What happens: Price goes up to +1R, I move stop to breakeven, it reverses
My action: Accept the breakeven stop out
Result: $0 profit, $0 loss, commission only
Probability: ~15%
MINDSET: This is a WIN - you protected capital and lost nothing

SCENARIO 5: SMALL LOSS (The Planned Loss)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What happens: Price goes against me immediately, hits my stop
My action: Accept the loss, DO NOT re-enter immediately
Result: Lose planned risk amount (e.g., $20)
Probability: ~25%
MINDSET: This is the COST OF DOING BUSINESS. It's fine.

SCENARIO 6: BLACK SWAN (Gap Down / News Bomb)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What happens: Bad news overnight, price gaps below my stop
My action: Sell immediately at market open, accept larger loss
Result: Lose more than planned (maybe 2x my risk)
Probability: ~5%
PREVENTION: Don't hold small caps through earnings or major events

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    EXPECTED VALUE CALCULATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

This is how you know if a trade is WORTH TAKING:

Scenario 1: +$60 × 20% = +$12.00
Scenario 2: +$60 × 15% = +$9.00
Scenario 3: +$30 × 20% = +$6.00
Scenario 4: $0 × 15% = $0.00
Scenario 5: -$20 × 25% = -$5.00
Scenario 6: -$40 × 5% = -$2.00

EXPECTED VALUE = $12 + $9 + $6 + $0 - $5 - $2 = +$20 per trade

If EV is POSITIVE → Trade is worth taking
If EV is NEGATIVE → Skip the trade

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    PRE-TRADE CHECKLIST
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

□ I know my exact entry price
□ I know my exact stop price
□ I know my target prices
□ I calculated position size based on risk
□ I war-gamed all 6 scenarios
□ I know what I'll do in EACH scenario
□ Expected value is positive
□ I'm emotionally prepared to lose my risk amount
□ This trade fits my daily loss limit
□ I'm not revenge trading from a previous loss

IF ALL CHECKED → Execute the trade
IF ANY UNCHECKED → Do not trade
```

## REAL EXAMPLE: KDK WAR GAME

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    TRADE WAR GAME: KDK
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TICKER: KDK (Kodiak AI)
DATE: December 28, 2025
THESIS: AI infrastructure play, price above all moving averages,
        RSI healthy at 45, MACD turning bullish, daily trend UP

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    THE NUMBERS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Entry Price:        $10.10 (wait for pullback to VWAP)
Stop Loss Price:    $9.70 (below recent support)
Target 1 (1.5R):    $10.70 (+$0.60)
Target 2 (2R):      $10.90 (+$0.80)
Target 3 (3R):      $11.30 (+$1.20)

Risk per share:     $0.40 ($10.10 - $9.70)
Position Size:      50 shares ($20 risk ÷ $0.40)
Total Risk:         $20 (4% of $500 account)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    SCENARIO PLANNING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SCENARIO 1: PERFECT TRADE
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
KDK gets AI contract news, rips to $11.30
Action: Sell 25 shares at $10.70, 25 shares at $11.00
Result: +$52.50 profit

SCENARIO 2: MESSY WIN
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
KDK drops to $9.75 (near my stop), I sweat, then rallies to $10.90
Action: Trust stop, don't panic, sell at targets
Result: +$35 profit but stressful

SCENARIO 3: PARTIAL WIN
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
KDK goes to $10.50, I move stop to breakeven, sell 25 shares
Price comes back to $10.10, remaining shares stopped at breakeven
Action: Take the partial, accept breakeven on rest
Result: +$10 profit (25 × $0.40)

SCENARIO 4: BREAKEVEN
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
KDK goes to $10.50, I move stop to $10.10, price reverses
Stopped out at $10.10
Action: Accept it, find next trade
Result: $0

SCENARIO 5: SMALL LOSS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
KDK drops immediately, hits $9.70 stop
Action: Accept the loss, review thesis, DO NOT chase
Result: -$20

SCENARIO 6: GAP DOWN
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Bad AI sector news overnight, KDK opens at $9.20
Action: Sell immediately at market open
Result: -$45 (larger than planned, but survivable)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    DECISION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Expected Value: POSITIVE
Risk is acceptable: YES (4% of account)
Thesis is clear: YES
All scenarios planned: YES

→ TRADE IS APPROVED. Wait for entry at $10.10.
```

## THE TICKER SELECTION WAR GAME

Now let's war-game your TICKER SELECTION:

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
          SECTOR WAR GAME: WHICH SECTORS WIN IN 2026?
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SCENARIO A: TRUMP DELIVERS ON PROMISES
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What happens:
- Defense spending increases
- Deregulation helps AI/tech
- Domestic manufacturing push

WINNERS: Defense (RCAT, KTOS), AI Infra (VRT, CRDO), Nuclear (SMR, UEC)
LOSERS: China-dependent companies, green energy (political headwind)
PROBABILITY: 40%

SCENARIO B: GRIDLOCK / TRUMP BLOCKED
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What happens:
- Congress blocks major initiatives
- Status quo continues
- Market trades on fundamentals

WINNERS: Quality companies in any sector
LOSERS: Companies dependent on policy changes
PROBABILITY: 30%

SCENARIO C: RECESSION / RISK-OFF
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What happens:
- Economic slowdown
- Flight to safety
- Small caps crushed

WINNERS: Cash, bonds, defensive stocks
LOSERS: All your small caps (RCAT, SMR, IONQ, etc.)
PROBABILITY: 15%
ACTION: Cut position sizes, raise cash, wait

SCENARIO D: GEOPOLITICAL CRISIS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What happens:
- China/Taiwan escalation
- Middle East expansion
- Energy crisis

WINNERS: Defense (huge), Energy (oil, nuclear), Cybersecurity
LOSERS: Consumer discretionary, China-exposed tech
PROBABILITY: 10%

SCENARIO E: AI BUBBLE POPS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What happens:
- AI hype exceeds reality
- Big AI names crash
- Contagion to related sectors

WINNERS: Cash, non-tech value stocks
LOSERS: AI infra, semiconductors, anything AI-adjacent
PROBABILITY: 5%
ACTION: Have stops on all AI-related plays

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
          SECTOR ALLOCATION BASED ON SCENARIOS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Given these probabilities:

DEFENSE: Wins in A, B, D → 80% of scenarios → HEAVY ALLOCATION
NUCLEAR: Wins in A, D → 50% of scenarios → MEDIUM ALLOCATION
AI INFRA: Wins in A, B → 70% of scenarios but risk in E → MEDIUM ALLOCATION
QUANTUM: Wins in A, B → 70% of scenarios → MEDIUM ALLOCATION
SPACE: Wins in A, B, D → 80% of scenarios → MEDIUM ALLOCATION

CASH RESERVE: Always keep 20-30% cash for Scenario C
```
