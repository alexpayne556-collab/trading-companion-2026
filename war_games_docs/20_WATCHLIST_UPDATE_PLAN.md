# HOW FIDELITY HEATMAP WORKS WITH YOUR WATCHLISTS
# ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

## THE KEY INSIGHT:

When you click "Heatmap" next to a watchlist:
- Fidelity shows ALL tickers in that watchlist as colored boxes
- GREEN = up today
- RED = down today
- SIZE of box = relative market cap or volume (you can configure)

## HOW TO USE IT:

### MORNING ROUTINE:
1. Open Heatmap for "DEFENSE_TECHNOLOGY"
   - See at a glance: Are defense stocks green or red today?
   - Is RCAT moving? Is the whole sector moving?

2. Open Heatmap for "ENERGY_TRANSFORMATION"
   - Is nuclear hot today?
   - Are uranium miners moving together?

3. Open Heatmap for "QUANTUM_TECHNOLOGIES"
   - Any quantum news driving the sector?

### WHAT YOU'RE LOOKING FOR:
- **Sector green, your pick red** = Possible buy opportunity
- **Sector red, your pick green** = Your pick is strong, good sign
- **Everything green** = Risk on day, be aggressive
- **Everything red** = Risk off, be careful
