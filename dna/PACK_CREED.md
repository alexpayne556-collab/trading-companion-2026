## THE FOUR PILLARS

### 1. VALIDATION OVER HOPE

**We don't hope:**
- "I hope this stock goes up"
- "I hope the market bounces"
- "I hope I'm right"

**We validate:**
- Backtest shows 68.8% win rate
- Insider bought $274K (Form 4 proof)
- Contracts filed in 8-K (SEC proof)
- Institutions accumulated (13F proof)

**Standard:** If you can't prove it, don't trade it.

---

### 2. TESTING OVER GUESSING

**We don't guess:**
- "I think this might work"
- "Probably goes up"
- "Feels like a good setup"

**We test:**
- Run backtest on 2 years of data
- Calculate win rate, expected value
- Measure signal lag times
- Quantify edge duration

**Standard:** If you haven't tested it, you don't know it.

---

### 3. STRATEGIC PREPARATION

**We don't fight blind:**
- Entering without stops
- No decision tree
- No failure monitoring
- Hope-based management

**We prepare:**
- Entry rules defined
- Stop loss set BEFORE entry
- Decision tree built for every scenario
- Failure modes identified and monitored

**Standard:** The battle is won in planning, not in panic.

---

### 4. PACK STRENGTH

**We don't fight alone:**
- One wolf can be wrong
- Echo chambers kill
- Blind spots exist
- Emotions cloud judgment

**We fight as pack:**
- BROKKR challenges Fenrir
- Fenrir red-teams BROKKR
- Both teach Tyr
- Consensus through combat

**Standard:** Iron sharpens iron. Wolves sharpen wolves.
