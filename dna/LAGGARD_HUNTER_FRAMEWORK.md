   - If yes ‚Üí VALID

3. **When could it catch up?**

## THE SECRETS THE SCUMBAGS DON'T WANT YOU TO KNOW

### SECRET 1: VOLUME PRECEDES PRICE
- Before a stock moves, volume spikes
- Unusual volume = someone knows something
- Watch volume BEFORE the move, not after

### SECRET 2: OPTIONS FLOW SHOWS THE PLAN
- When institutions bet big on calls, they expect UP
- When they buy puts, they expect DOWN
- The strikes they choose = their price targets
- Brokkr's scanner catches this

### SECRET 3: INSIDERS KNOW
- When CEO buys stock with their OWN money (Form 4, Code P), they believe
- When they sell, they don't
- Insider BUYING is a signal. Insider selling is noise (they always sell for taxes/diversification)

### SECRET 4: THE MORNING TRAP
- 9:30-10:00 AM is CHAOS
- Retail buys at open, gets trapped
- Smart money waits for the 10 AM dip
- THAT'S WHY. You're buying the retail trap.

### SECRET 5: POWER HOUR IS REAL
- 3:00-4:00 PM is when institutions position for overnight
- If they're buying at 3:30, they expect good news overnight
- Watch the 3:30 direction

### SECRET 6: CATALYSTS ARE PRICED IN... MOSTLY
- "Buy the rumor, sell the news" is REAL
- The move happens BEFORE the announcement
- By the time YOU see the news, it's too late
- That's why you need to be 1-2 days EARLY

### SECRET 7: SECTORS ROTATE IN ORDER
- Money doesn't go everywhere at once
- It flows: Leader ‚Üí Second place ‚Üí Third place ‚Üí Laggard
- The LAGGARD in a hot sector is the LAST to run
- That's your window
