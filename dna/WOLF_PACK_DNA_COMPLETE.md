## Position Sizing Rules

```
ACCOUNT SIZE: $1,280
MAX RISK PER TRADE: 5% = $64
STOP LOSS: 10% below entry
SHARES = Risk Amount / (Entry - Stop)
```

**Example:**
```
Stock: BBAI @ $5.40
Stop: $4.86 (10% below)
Risk per share: $0.54
Max shares: $64 / $0.54 = 118 shares
Position value: $637
```
