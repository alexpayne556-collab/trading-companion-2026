## THE PATTERN: COILED SPRING

### Setup:
1. **Micro-cap** (<$100M, ideally <$25M)
2. **Intraday spike** 15-20%+ (usually mid-morning)
3. **Fade from high** 10-20% (profit-taking, shorts pile in)
4. **Volume concentration** 30%+ in spike hour
5. **Still closes green** or near highs

### Why It Works:
- Morning spike = news leaked / catalyst coming
- Fade = profit-takers exit, shorts get greedy
- After-hours = news officially drops
- Shorts trapped, explosion happens

### The Tell:
**"Spike + Fade = Coil"** - Like compressing a spring. Release happens AH.
