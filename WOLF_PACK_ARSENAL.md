- **Contract News:** DOE/DOD/NASA/hyperscaler deals (‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê Tier 1)
- **Sector Rotation:** Money flowing INTO sector
- **Relative Strength:** Outperforming sector average
- **Volume Spike:** >2x average with positive price action
- **Near Highs:** Within 10% of 52-week high + momentum
- **Earnings Beat:** Multiple quarters beating + guidance raise

### ‚ö†Ô∏è BEARISH SIGNALS

- **Sector Rotation OUT:** Money leaving sector
- **Declining Strength:** Underperforming sector
