- L1 RADAR: Check indices, VIX, sector heatmaps, news
- L2 FILTER: Load hot sector watchlist, sort by Vol % Avg, pick top candidates
- L3 SNIPER: Mark support/resistance, entry/stop/target, calculate R:R and size
- FINAL CHECK: Pre-fill order, confirm thesis, wait for open

### MARKET OPEN (9:30 AM - 11:00 AM ET)
- Watch first 15 min, no trades
- Execute only if setup holds, set stop loss immediately
- Manage positions, move stop to breakeven at +1R

### MIDDAY (11:00 AM - 2:00 PM ET)
- Manage positions, avoid boredom trades

### AFTERNOON (2:00 PM - 4:00 PM ET)
- Refresh, manage positions, close day trades by 3:45 PM
- Review P&L, prepare for tomorrow

### POSITION MANAGEMENT
- +1R: Move stop to breakeven
- +1.5R to +2R: Sell 50%
- +2R to +3R: Sell another 25%, trail stop
- Stop hit: Exit, no re-entry

### DAILY LOSS LIMIT
- Max daily loss: 5% of account
- If hit: Close all, walk away

### THE 5 RULES
1. Always have a stop loss
2. Never move stop further away
3. Take partials at targets
4. Cut losers fast
5. Let winners run (with protection)
