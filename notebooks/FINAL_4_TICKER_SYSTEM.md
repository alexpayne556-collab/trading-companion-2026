# THE 4-TICKER TIMING SYSTEM

Based on 60-day backtest of 6 timing strategies across 10 tickers.

## The Brutal Truth
- Only 11 of 60 combinations were profitable
- Win rates are around 50%
- Edge comes from gains > losses, not win rate

## The 4 Winners

| Ticker | Strategy | 60-Day Return | $1,300 → |
|--------|----------|---------------|----------|
| ASTS | Open → 10:30-11:00 | +16.9% | $1,520 |
| APLD | Open → 10:30-11:00 | +15.6% | $1,503 |
| BE | First Hour → Power Hour | +9.2% | $1,420 |
| NVVE | Morning Dip → Afternoon | +4.4% | $1,357 |

## Rules

### ASTS & APLD (Same Strategy)
- **BUY:** At market open (9:30-9:45 AM)
- **SELL:** Between 10:30-11:00 AM
- **Hold time:** ~1 hour

### BE
- **BUY:** During first hour (9:30-10:30 AM)
- **SELL:** During power hour (3:00-4:00 PM)
- **Hold time:** ~5-6 hours

### NVVE
- **BUY:** During morning dip (9:30-10:00 AM)
- **SELL:** Afternoon (1:00-2:00 PM)
- **Hold time:** ~3-4 hours
